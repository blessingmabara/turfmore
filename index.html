About Us

A company of first choice. For all borehole siting, borehole drilling and borehole pumb installations and also, drip irrigation system installation and solar systems installation.

Turfmore is company which provides various services across the country, from drip irrigation, solar systems and borehole installations. Remenber the days when borehole siting was used to be done using the traditional copper wire. It was very difficult and challenging to find the best spot to drill a borehole, and in some cases, the borehole could be drilled but later on, the water is nomore. But do not worry, because with Turfmore we use high advanced and more efficient tools such as a three-axis magnetometer which measures the azimuthal direction of the borehole and a dual tilt sensor which provides information about the iclination.
